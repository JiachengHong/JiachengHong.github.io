Research

I study Physical Oceanography and Atmospheric Science, trying to understand the relationship between intensity and structure in tropical cyclones.

  • rapid intensification of tropical cyclone

    • Rapid intensification (RI) of TC is defined as an intensification of more than 30 knots in 24 h, most category 4 and 5 hurricanes underwent RI at least once during their lifetimes. A better understanding of the relationship between intensity/intensification and structure in tropical cyclones could improve our understanding of the mechanisms involved in TC RI.
